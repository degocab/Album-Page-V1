Altered State

Tesseract

May 27, 2013

New album from Tesseract showcasing their talents as fore-runners in the new djent genre. With this new progressive album they set a new bar for other aspiring bands in this genre.

Band Members

Ashe O'Hara - Lead Vocals
Acle Kahney - Guitar
James Monteith - Guitar
Jay Postones - Drums
Amos Williams - Bass
